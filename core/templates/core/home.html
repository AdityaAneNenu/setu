About the Initiative

The Pradhan Mantri Anusuchit Jaati Abhyudaya Yojana (PM-AJAY) aims to holistically develop Scheduled Caste majority villages into Adarsh Grams by identifying and resolving infrastructure and service gaps.

🎯

Gap Identification

Comprehensive assessments identify critical deficits across public services and infrastructure.

📊

Progress Tracking

Live monitoring helps teams prioritize interventions and track implementation outcomes.

🤝

Community Engagement

Local participation ensures development reflects real village needs and priorities.
